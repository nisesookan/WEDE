ABOUT

Our Mission

"To empower underprivileged youth with digital skills and opportunities that foster personal and professional growth in the technology sector."

OUR INITIATIVES

Empowering Through Training

At YETT, we are dedicated to bridging the digital divide by offering a range of initiatives designed to equip young people with the necessary skills to thrive in a technology-driven world. Our key initiatives include.

- Tech Workshops: Regular hands-on workshops covering various aspects of technology, from basic coding to advanced software development.

- Online Learning Platforms: Accessible online courses that allow students to learn at their own pace, anytime and anywhere.

- Mentorship Programs: One-on-one and group mentoring sessions with industry professionals to provide guidance and career advice.

EMPOWERMENT PROGRAMS

Our empowerment programs are at the heart of what we do, providing essential training and support to help youth succeed:

- Introduction to Coding: A foundational course to kickstart a career in programming.

- Web Development Fundamentals: Training in creating and maintaining websites with modern technologies.

- Graphic Design and Multimedia: Skills development for creating compelling visual content.

- Data Analysis and Visualization: Techniques for analyzing data and creating impactful visual representations.

- Cybersecurity Basics:* Essentials for protecting information and understanding online security threats.

- Advanced Coding and Software Development:In-depth training for those looking to pursue advanced programming careers.

COMMUNITY PARTNERSHIPS

We believe in the power of collaboration and work closely with various partners to enhance our impact:

- Tech Companies: Partnering with leading tech firms to provide internships, job placements, and real-world project experiences.

- Educational Institutions: Collaborating with schools and universities to integrate our programs into their curriculum and expand our reach.

- Local Organizations: Working with community groups to identify and support youth in need of digital skills training.
